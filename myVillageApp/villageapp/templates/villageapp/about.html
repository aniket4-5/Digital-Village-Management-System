Rajiv Nagar Jhiri

Hello ! ... Ram Ram ... ! this is sarpanch of Rajiv Nagar Jhiri and Gram Panchyat . We welcome you to official website of Rajiv Nagar Jhiri .Rajiv Nagar is a village from Hisar district of Haryana. It is under administration of Gram Panchayat.Main occupation is agriculture.There is need big development in Rajiv Nagar from road to electricity and education. As time is changing we are also trying to be computerized from the tedious and time - consuming paperwork. This website is the result of one of our goals to improve our administration by shifting from paper to computer. It will not only reduce corruption but make things faster which is a matter of big stress to our resident . This website primarily focuses on announcements and complaints by our residents . They can do complains online . Now they don't have to walk long 2 km just for filing a complaint . But we are also going to add a picture gallery of our village. Also also share glorious history of our village . This will help in attaracting tourists and people from outside will be more respectful to our village .
